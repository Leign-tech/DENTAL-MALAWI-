About Dental Malawi

Your trusted source for clear, reliable dental treatment information across Malawi. We connect patients with central hospitals and clinics so you can make informed decisions about your oral health.

Our Mission

Dental Malawi was created to solve one problem: finding clear dental information is hard. We gather treatment options, estimated prices, and hospital locations in one place so Malawians can access care with confidence.

What We Do

We provide up-to-date information on dental services available at central hospitals and private clinics nationwide. From tooth extraction to braces, we list what’s available and guide you to the right facility near you in Blantyre, Lilongwe, Mzuzu and beyond.

Our Commitment

We are not a clinic. We are an information platform. All prices and services listed are for general guidance only. We recommend you contact the hospital directly for confirmation and booking. Your health and trust matter to us.
